Discover the beauty and utility of exquisite gemstones

Emerald

Found in Colombia, Zambia, and Brazil. Known for its vivid green hue and symbolizes rebirth and love.

Garnet

Found in India, Sri Lanka, and Africa. Believed to promote health and creativity.

Opal

Found in Australia and Ethiopia. Celebrated for its play-of-color and symbolizes hope.

Sapphire

Found in Sri Lanka, Myanmar, and Madagascar. Known for its royal blue color and promotes wisdom.

Diamond

Found in South Africa, Russia, and Canada. Renowned for its brilliance and symbolizes strength.

Amethyst

Found in Brazil, Zambia, and Uruguay. Known for its calming properties and deep purple hue.

Aquamarine

Found in Pakistan, Brazil, and Nigeria. Symbolizes tranquility and courage.

Ruby

Found in Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam. Known as the stone of passion and energy.

Peridot

Found in Egypt, Pakistan, and the USA. Symbolizes prosperity and warmth.

Agate

Found in Brazil, India, and Madagascar. Known for stability and grounding energy.
